EXAMPLE SCENARIOS

Special Assessment
Master pool balance: Rs. 232,500
Generator breaks down
Generator replacement cost: Rs. 250,000
Shortfall: Rs. 250,000 - Rs. 232,500 = Rs. 17,500
Now you need to charge residents for this Rs. 17,500
POSTED A SPECIAL ASSESSMENT
{title: "Emergency Generator Replacement",
  reason: "Old generator failed. Replacement needed for building operation",
  required_amount: 250000,
  current_balance: 232500}

  System calculates:
  shortfall: 250000 - 232500 = 17500
  occupied_units: 18 (count of units where status != 'Vacant')
  per_unit_amount: ceil(17500 / 18) = 972.22 ≈ 973
